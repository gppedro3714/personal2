CASE_01

Differential Geometry

Global structure of Lorentzian spacetimes; causality theory (global hyperbolicity, chronology protection); orientability and its physical consequences (chirality, spin structures); the geometry of spacelike foliations and the initial value problem in General Relativity.

CASE_02

Topological Methods in Quantum Cosmology

Superspace and the space of 3-geometries; fiber bundles and foliations over parameter spaces; vacuum tunneling and instanton transitions between topologically distinct universes; the string landscape as a discrete topological phase space.

CASE_03

Formal Ontology of Physical Theories

Structural realism and the mathematical foundations of physical models; using category theory and sheaf theory to formalize inter-theoretic relations; model-theoretic approaches to multiverse scenarios and simulation hypotheses.
